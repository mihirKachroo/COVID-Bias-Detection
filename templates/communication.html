Submission

Inspiration
A media critic inveighs against the bias toward centrism, which inspires us to come up with this project idea. It’s vital to democracy and has performed admirably in the age of Trump. But we also believe in reflection and criticism.The media isn't just biased toward the center. It often confuses the center with views that are actually those of the affluent. . Our code changes that however because our code takes the article published and finds out how biased it is. Whether it is left-leaning(Liberal bias) or right-leaning(Conservative bias).

What It Does
For any news article.Take theURL of that article and put it into the user interface build. Our code will run the algorithm through the corpus .Once the Algorithm completes it’s processing it will give you whether the article is biased or unbiased.It will also check whether the article is Liberal bias or conservative bias by checking it’s left leaning and right leaning.

How We Built It
We built it by using a bunch of different algorithms including Natural Language Processing (NLP), HTML and Flask.

Challenges We Ran Into
We had to combine a few NLP and classification algorithms which took a lot of time for processing and made us kind of impatient.we need to be aware of the political perspective of the sources used in a story. Media over-rely on “official” (government, corporate and establishment think tank) sources. So finding a right and correct corpus for this task is very important.

Accomplishments that we're proud of
We are proud that we finished the entire program even though it took a couple of hours we did it.

What We Learned
We learned that there is a way to help tell everyone that not all news articles are completely unbiased of course some are but most are not.Visual presentation plays a huge role in people believing in fake news content.
